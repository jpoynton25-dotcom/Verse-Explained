## Decision Guide

### When to expand a page
- Impressions are rising week over week and average position is between 8 and 20.
- Page gets clicks, but users likely need deeper sections (add practical steps, examples, and internal links).
- Page starts ranking for adjacent long-tail queries you can cover with additional sub-sections.

### When to improve intro/FAQ
- Impressions increase but CTR stays low (for example <2-3%).
- Query intent in Search Console looks different from your current intro framing.
- FAQ questions do not match real queries showing in GSC.

### When to leave a page alone
- Newly published and still in the first 14 days.
- Low data volume (very few impressions/clicks) without obvious quality issues.
- Stable improvement in impressions/position without intervention.

### When to build a supporting page instead
- The page begins ranking for a distinct sub-intent that deserves its own URL.
- Adding more content would dilute the current page's core intent.
- Multiple related pages can form a stronger internal-link cluster (hub -> child -> sibling).
