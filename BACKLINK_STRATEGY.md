# Backlink and Authority Strategy (No Fabricated Links)

## Objective
Build topical authority for VerseExplained.com through high-quality, relevant links and strong internal hub structure.

## Linkable Assets to Create
- Deep guides: "How to Read a Bible Verse in Context" and "Bible Study for Beginners".
- Downloadables: topical scripture PDFs (anxiety, grief, strength) with concise explanation notes.
- Visual assets: scripture explainer infographics for Pinterest and blog embeds.
- Data-style posts: monthly "Most searched Bible verses and questions" roundups.

## Outreach Opportunities
- Guest posts on Christian blogs and faith-oriented resource sites.
- Inclusion on church and ministry resource pages.
- Christian directories with editorial review.
- Collaboration with scripture-focused YouTube channels and podcast hosts.
- Quote participation in faith/lifestyle roundups.

## Supporting Channels
- Pinterest pins linking to verse hubs and need-based pages.
- YouTube Shorts with verse context leading to article pages.
- Email newsletter linking to pillar pages and new explainers.

## Internal Authority Building
- Keep each hub page updated with newest child pages.
- Add related links from every child page to 3-5 relevant pages.
- Add contextual links from terms/people pages to verse meaning pages.
- Prioritize links into top commercial-intent informational pages with stable search demand.

## Quality Guardrails
- No paid spam links.
- No private blog networks.
- No artificial anchor stuffing.
- Focus on relevance, editorial standards, and long-term trust.
